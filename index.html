The Hospital Advantage

100% Practical Training in Our Parent Hospital

Unlike institutions that rely entirely on simulated labs, TSS Medical Education students train directly inside our operational parent hospital starting from their **first year**.

Immediate Clinical Exposure

Students participate in active diagnostic setups and operation theatres right from the very first semester.

Advanced Diagnostics Laboratories

Our testing labs feature high-throughput haematology counters, biochemistry, and microbiology setups.

Multi-Specialty Operation Theatres
