BSc Medical Laboratory Technology

Unlock the mysteries of medical diagnosis! As a Medical Laboratory Technologist, you are the backbone of clinical decision-making. You will learn to perform intricate biochemical, haematological, immunological, and microbiological tests on blood and tissues, providing crucial data that doctors rely on to save lives every day.

Duration

3 Yrs + 1 Yr Internship

Eligibility

12th Science (PCMB) - Min 45%

Core Skills You Will Gain

Haematology & Blood Analysis Microbiology & Culture Preparation Clinical Biochemistry Systems Automated Pathology Equipment Sterilization & Lab Safety Controls

Global Career Opportunities

Multi-Specialty Hospital Labs
Independent Diagnostic Centers
State/Central Govt Recruitment
International Placements (UK/Gulf)

Expected Salary Spectrum

₹ 2.5 LPA - ₹ 15+ LPA (Abroad)
Fresher (2 - 3 Lakhs/Yr) Mid-Level (5 - 8 Lakhs/Yr) Senior / Abroad (15+ Lakhs/Yr)

Course Curriculum (Degree Blueprint)

Year 1

Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry Foundations. Clinical logs.

Year 2

Clinical Pathology, Systemic Microbiology, Blood Bank logs.

Year 3

Advanced Cytology, Diagnostic Serology, Quality Controls.

Year 4

Full 1-Year Clinical Internship at Parent Hospital.

Interested in BSc Medical Laboratory Technology?

Limited seats available for the 2026-27 batch. Train in our operational multi-specialty hospital.

See the invisible! Imaging technologists utilize sophisticated radiation and scanning technologies to map the internal anatomical systems of patients. You will learn to operate state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment including X-Ray systems, CT scans, MRI suites, and diagnostic Ultrasounds under the clinical supervision of veteran radiologists.

Duration

3 Yrs + 1 Yr Internship

Eligibility

12th Science (PCMB) - Min 45%

Core Skills You Will Gain

X-Ray Radiography Operations MRI and CT Scan Calibration Radiation Safety & Lead Shielding Digital Medical Image Processing Patient Positioning & Comfort Controls

Global Career Opportunities

Radiology & Imaging Wings
Cancer Radiotherapy Centers
Scanning Center Partnerships
Global Medical Imaging Corporates

Expected Salary Spectrum

₹ 3.0 LPA - ₹ 18+ LPA (Abroad)
Fresher (3 - 4 Lakhs/Yr) Mid-Level (6 - 9 Lakhs/Yr) Senior / Abroad (18+ Lakhs/Yr)

Course Curriculum (Degree Blueprint)

Year 1

Physics of Radiation, Human Anatomy, Physiology fundamentals.

Year 2

X-Ray Positioning and Radiographic Technique logs.

Year 3

MRI Systems, Computed Tomography, Ultrasonography logs.

Full 1-Year Clinical Internship at Parent Hospital.

Work at the heart of surgical operations! Operation Theatre and Anaesthesia Technologists are key members of the surgical team. You will master operation room sterilization, manage complex surgical instruments, prep anaesthetic agents, and monitor patient vitals using advanced life-support equipment during real live surgeries.

Duration

3 Yrs + 1 Yr Internship

Eligibility

12th Science (PCMB) - Min 45%

Core Skills You Will Gain

OT Sterile Field Setup Surgical Instrument Coordination Anaesthetic Agent Preparation Cardiorespiratory Vital Monitoring Emergency ICU Equipment setups

Global Career Opportunities

Major Operating Rooms
Intensive Care Units (ICU)
Emergency Casualty Trauma
Global Surgical Device firms

Expected Salary Spectrum

₹ 2.8 LPA - ₹ 16+ LPA (Abroad)
Fresher (2.8 - 3.5 Lakhs/Yr) Mid-Level (5 - 8.5 Lakhs/Yr) Senior / Abroad (16+ Lakhs/Yr)

Sterility, Basic Anatomy, Nursing Foundations, Pathology.

Operating Room layout protocols, Basic Anaesthesia pharmacology.

Bachelors in Hospital Administration (BHA)

Bridge management and medicine! **BHA is a unique healthcare management degree that welcomes students from ALL STREAMS — Arts, Commerce, and Science alike!** While most healthcare courses are locked behind Science prerequisites, BHA is open to all. You will master operational planning, clinical quality protocols, healthcare finance, hospital human resource systems, and supply chains in medical institutes.

Duration

3 Years (No Internship Year)

Eligibility

12th Std (Arts, Commerce, Sci) - 45%

Core Skills You Will Gain

Hospital Operations Management Healthcare HR & Talent systems Clinical Billing & Insurance systems Quality Accreditation (NABH) Medical Supply Chain logistics

Global Career Opportunities

Administrative Officers
Quality & Accreditation Leads
Medical Billing Superintendents
Healthcare IT Project Coordinators

Expected Salary Spectrum

₹ 3.0 LPA - ₹ 14+ LPA (Abroad)
Fresher (3 - 4.5 Lakhs/Yr) Mid-Level (5 - 8.5 Lakhs/Yr) Senior / Abroad (14+ Lakhs/Yr)

Course Curriculum (Degree Blueprint)

Year 1

Hospital layouts, Healthcare history, General Accounting.

Year 2

Hospital Human Resources, Ward inventory logistics, Billing.

Year 3

Quality standardizations, Medical Audits, Project logs.

Non-Science Student looking for a Medical Career?

Enroll in BHA. Arts, Science and Commerce candidates are all welcome to apply.

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)

Accelerate your diagnostic testing career! DMLT provides high-speed, intensive training to prepare you for laboratory testing routines. You will learn blood cell counting, slide staining, culture inoculations, and the use of chemical reagents under state board medical regulations.

Duration

2 Yrs (Post 12th) / 3 Yrs (Post 10th)

Eligibility

10th Std / 12th Sci (Flexible Entry)

Core Skills You Will Gain

Basic Blood Slide Staining Culture Media Inoculation Chemical Pathology basics Reagent Mixing & Storage logs

Global Career Opportunities

Clinical Lab Technicians
Blood Bank assistants
Health Camp Technicians
Pathology Clinic associates

Expected Salary Spectrum

₹ 1.8 LPA - ₹ 8+ LPA
Fresher (1.8 - 2.5 Lakhs/Yr) Mid-Level (4 - 5.5 Lakhs/Yr) Senior (8+ Lakhs/Yr)

Course Curriculum (Diploma Blueprint)

Year 1

Elementary Anatomy, Lab hygiene, Serology methods.

Year 2

Active lab analysis training, Pipetting logs.

Year 3

Clinical internships and serology placements (10th Entry only).

Interested in DMLT?

Apply post 10th or 12th standard. Accelerated training inside our parent hospital.
